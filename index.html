Who is Salehe Rehani?

Salehe Rehani is a Montreal-based rapper, singer, songwriter and model who grew up on the westcoast in British Colombia before returning east to Montreal for university. Over the years Salehe has written and recorded hundreds of songs, and written and sold songs to dozens of customers aorund the world. Aside from music his hobbies and intrests include playing basketball, coding, studying urban planning, doing business and learning about economics.
